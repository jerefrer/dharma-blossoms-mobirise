A Vision of Reconciliation

In these challenging times of environmental crisis and alienation, anger about the past, and fear for our future, we are profoundly fortunate to have a path back to kindness, to friendship, and ultimately, to peace. It is an ancient path that begins with our innate curiosity about our own mind; it is a journey, that always relies on a staff of kindness.

With the Buddha’s teachings as our guide, together, we can follow this path of reconciliation within ourselves, with each other, and with the world.
